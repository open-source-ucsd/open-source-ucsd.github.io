FAQs

1) I am stuck on a tutorial or challenge, where can I go to get help?

There is a discord channel set up for anyone who needs online help. Feel free to send us a message! Link to Discord

2) Do I need programming experience for Open Source?

Absolutely not! In each of the weekly emails, we will provide you some learning tools. The tutorials/challenges will also guide you step by step.

3) Can I sign up late?

Yes! Please fill out the mail sign up form.

4) How can I unsubscribe to the weekly emails?

Send us a quick message! We completely understand.
